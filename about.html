Void Break 2

Void Break is an arcade style game based heavily off of Asteroids with some inspiration from Nova Drift and Bosconian. It's built in JavaScript with Canvas for graphics and Howler for sound effects.

The game plays very similar to Asteroids in the basic concepts and objective but varies in several ways. The first of which is the movement style, where in Asteroids there is no friction whatsoever, here there is a good bit of friction. This produces a very floaty movement system that is a bit more comfortable to control while still placing emphasis on the movement and thinking a step ahead of where the character is.

Another deviation is the camera and world. In both Asteroids and Nova Drift the world is confined to the edges of the view and upon hitting the edge, the character is warped to the other side. In Void Break, the world is much larger with the camera staying centered on the player but it still keeps the ability to wrap around the world seamlessly by using a looping background and showing the other side of the world over the edges.

The health system uses a shield and health bar instead of lives and the shield starts to replenish after several seconds of not taking damage.

The asteroids are spawned with a size and frequency dependant on the current score and the number of asteroids already in existence. The shape is randomly generated for each asteroid and velocity as well as initial placement are random. Upon breaking an asteroid, it will fragment into others if it is large enough and increment the score.
